# Updating Electrode

This page will guide you through updating Electrode.

## Method I: Automatic

### Requirements:

- Python 3.11+
- The latest version of [ElectrodeUpdater](https://github.com/SamtendoNetwork/ElectrodeUpdater/releases/latest)
- 1-2 minutes

1. Insert your SD Card into your PC
1. Run ElectrodeUpdater.py
1. Fill in the correct drive letter
1. Done! Safely eject your SD Card and place it back into your Wii U

## Method II: Manual

1. Insert your SD Card into your PC
1. Download the latest version of [Electrode](https://github.com/SamtendoNetwork/Electrode/releases/latest) **(Both files!)**
1. Navigate to **SD:/wiiu/environments/aroma/plugins** and delete **Electrode-samtendo.wps**
1. Insert the new **Electrode-samtendo.wps** into there
1. Navigate to **SD:/wiiu/environments/aroma/modules** and delete **Electrode-samtendo.wms**
1. Insert the new **Electrode-samtendo.wms** into there
1. Done! Safely eject your SD Card and place it back into your Wii U
